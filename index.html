How it works

Five steps, one unforgettable gift.

No technical skills needed. Works with any WhatsApp chat export — couples, friends, families, groups.

01

Export your chat

Open WhatsApp → Chat → More → Export Chat → Without media. Download the .txt.

02

🔒 Open our analyzer

Drop the .txt into our browser tool. It runs locally — verify in DevTools Network tab (stays empty). Your raw chat never uploads.

03

Curate your moments

Toggle ✅ on the quotes, milestones, motifs you love. Sensitive items (OTPs, phones, banks) are auto-locked 🔒. Download a small manifest file.

04

Send us the manifest

Send the small JSON manifest (NOT your chat) via DM. We design from your approved moments. That's all we ever see.

05

Receive your gift

Story cards, PDF keepsake, or a cinematic private website — delivered as a private download link. 5 days total.
