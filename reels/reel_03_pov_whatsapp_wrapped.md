## Production Notes

- This reel lives or dies on how believable the "receive" perspective feels. If you can mock up a real-looking WhatsApp message with cards attached, do it — even a screen recording of a Canva mockup opened in Safari works.
- The POV format works best when the viewer genuinely forgets they're watching an ad. Lean into that.
- Voiceover tone: soft, slightly surprised/delighted — like you're narrating as the person receiving it. Not salesy.
- At Scene 5 (privacy beat), keep it casual. Don't over-explain. "The privacy thing is actually real" is more believable than a formal disclosure.
- CTA flip at the end (receiver POV → give it to someone) is intentional — it makes the viewer think of a specific person. That's what drives DMs.
- Audio: use a trending lo-fi or soft indie track. Something with a "warm surprise" vibe — not dramatic, not overly romantic.
- Aspect ratio: 9:16. Keep text out of the bottom 15% (covered by IG caption bar).
