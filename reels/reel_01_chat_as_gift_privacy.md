## Production Notes

- Use dark/moody lo-fi beat as background audio (trending on IG, no lyrics)
- DevTools network tab scene is the proof moment — slow it down, hold for 2 seconds
- Cards scroll: slow pan, not a fast montage — let each card register
- Text: white on dark, large enough to read at 0.5x viewing speed
- Do NOT use face-cam for this one — screen record + text is cleaner and more trustworthy for a privacy-angle reel
