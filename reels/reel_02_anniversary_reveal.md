## Hook (First 3 Seconds)

**Visual:** Close-up of someone's hands opening an envelope or unboxing a small card set. Slightly blurred, building anticipation.
**On-screen text (large, centered):**
> "She cried when she saw this."

**Why it works:** First-person social proof framing. Triggers curiosity before the product is revealed. "What is 'this'?" forces the viewer to stay.

## Production Notes

- The physical card reveal in Scene 1–2 is the emotional anchor — if you can print even one sample card before recording, use it. If not, use a high-res mockup displayed on a tablet/screen in soft light.
- No face-cam needed. Hands + cards + music does the work.
- Audio: use a slow, emotional instrumental (no lyrics). IG trending: search "emotional lo-fi" or "cinematic piano."
- Pacing should feel deliberate and slightly slow — this is not a fast-cut reel. The emotion needs space.
- The privacy beat (Scene 6) should feel like a reassuring aside, not the main message — keep it brief but visible.
