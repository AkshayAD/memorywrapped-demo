Trust & privacy

Why clients feel safe using this.

Privacy is the biggest objection. These are our built-in answers.

No WhatsApp account access

Clients export the chat themselves and share only the .txt file. We never touch their app or account.

Quote approval before delivery

Every message that might appear in the keepsake is shown to the client first. Nothing is included without sign-off.

Raw chat deleted after generation

The .txt export is used only during the generation step and deleted automatically. We do not store it.

Private link, not public page

The output is a private download or private signed link. It is never indexed or shared publicly by default.

Synthetic demos only in marketing

We use fictional synthetic chats for all marketing and demos. No real customer data ever appears publicly.

Delete on request, 24h

Clients can request full deletion at any time. We purge everything within 24 hours, no questions asked.

Common questions

We handle Hinglish and mixed-language WhatsApp exports well. The parser reads the message structure, not language-specific keywords only. We'll show you a demo if needed.
